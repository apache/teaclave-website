Contributors

Apache Teaclave™ adopts The Apache Way. Everyone is more than welcome to contribute and make the project better. We add tag along with committer name to show areas that they are familiar with. We do encourage everyone to work anything they are interested in.

Please contact the community if you'd like to become a member of regular committers or PPMC of Teaclave.

Project Management Committee​

The role of Project Management Committee (PMC) is to help to govern the project and is directly responsible for the oversight of Teaclave.
